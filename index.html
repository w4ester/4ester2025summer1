Clearwater Beach – 5‑Day Itinerary with Live Links

Age 6

  1. Morning play at South Beach Playground ➜ Dinner at Frenchy’s Rockaway Grill ➜ Pier 60 Sunset Festival.
  2. Hands‑on fun at the Clearwater Marine Aquarium ➜ Pirate cruise on Captain Memo’s Pirate Ship ➜ Ice cream on Mandalay Ave.
  3. Ferry to Caladesi Island State Park ➜ Picnic lunch ➜ Hotel pool time.
  4. Great Explorations Children’s Museum in St. Pete ➜ Afternoon Sea Screamer Dolphin Cruise.
  5. Tide‑pooling at Sand Key Park ➜ Souvenir stop ➜ Depart.

Age 12

  4. Art day at The Dalí Museum & St. Pete murals ➜ Little Toot Dolphin Tour.
  5. Morning parasailing session ➜ Pack & depart.

Age 14

  1. Jet‑ski adventure with H2O Watersports ➜ Fishing clinic on Pier 60.
  2. Teen volunteer shift at CMA ➜ Beach volleyball at CB Volleyball Courts.
  3. Cycling the Pinellas Trail ➜ Sunset photo workshop.
  4. Thrill‑day at Busch Gardens Tampa.
  5. Surf lesson via Clearwater Surf School ➜ Depart.
